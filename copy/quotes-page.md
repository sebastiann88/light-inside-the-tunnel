## COPY NOTES

- **The page header and CTA band do all the copywriting work.** The quotes themselves are the content — they don't need framing or commentary. The page is a gallery, not an essay.
- **"Words from the tunnel"** is evocative without being cryptic. It connects to the book title and positions the quotes as things found in darkness — which makes them feel discovered, not curated.
- **"These are just fragments. The book is the whole."** — This is the softest possible sell. It acknowledges the value of what you've just read while pointing to something larger. No pressure.
- **Share functionality:** MVP can be simple (copy text to clipboard). Phase 2 can add styled image generation for social sharing.
