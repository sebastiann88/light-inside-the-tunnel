## COPY NOTES

- **Every CTA is warm.** No "Submit," "Buy now," "Learn more," or "Contact us." The language should feel like an invitation from a person, not a command from a website.
- **404 page uses the tunnel metaphor.** It turns a dead end into a moment that fits the site's world.
- **Privacy page is written in the site's voice** but stays factually accurate. It should feel refreshingly human compared to standard privacy policies.
- **Newsletter success message** ("Welcome in.") is short, warm, and matches the design system. Two words. No fanfare — just a quiet door opening.
- **Footer contact line** ("Got a thought? Say hello.") is open-ended and inviting without promising a response time or creating a support expectation.
