### The Book in a Breath

This is a book about the gap between who you say you are and who you actually become — day after day, choice after choice. It's for anyone who's ever sat alone at 3 AM wondering if they're living the life they meant to live.

It's not a self-help manual. It's not an academic treatise. It's a conversation — honest, sometimes funny, always human — about what it means to live consistently with your own heart.
